      Opt-Out Notice

      July 1, 2023

      This NOTICE OF RIGHT TO OPT-OUT OF SALE OR SHARING OF PERSONAL INFORMATION (“OPT-OUT NOTICE”) is provided to inform residents of states with applicable consumer privacy laws of their right to direct Nissan North America, Inc. (“Nissan” or “we”) to restrict certain uses of residents’ personal information.

      In reviewing this Opt-Out Notice, you also may want to review the Nissan Privacy Notice, which describes our privacy policy, including with respect to the sale, sharing, use and disclosure of your information. 

      Specific Information for California Residents

      Nissan is providing resident of the State of California with this Opt-Out Notice to comply with the California Privacy Rights Act of 2020 (“CPRA”) (which amended the California Consumer Privacy Act), and other California privacy laws. Any terms defined in the CPRA have the same meaning when used in this Opt-Out Notice.

      Under the CPRA, California consumers have the right to direct businesses not to sell or share their personal information if the businesses otherwise would. This is known as the “right to opt-out.” Note, however, that with respect to California’s Right to Limit the Use or Disclosure of Sensitive Personal Information, Nissan’s usage and disclosure of Sensitive Personal Information is for exempted purposes and not subject to Requests to Limit the Use or Disclosure of such information.

      Specific Information for Residents of Other States

      Nissan is providing residents of other states with consumer privacy laws with this Opt-Out Notice to comply with those states’ associated notice requirements. Under most states’ consumer privacy laws, residents have the right to direct businesses not to process their personal data for purposes of one or more of the following: (i) targeted advertising, (ii) selling, or (iii) profiling in furtherance of decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects concerning the consumer. This is known as the “right to opt-out.” 

      How to Opt-Out

      You may opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information and/or limit the use or disclosure of Sensitive Personal Information (as applicable depending on your residency) by contacting Nissan’s Data Privacy line 1-877-647-2272 or by completing this web form:

      CONSUMER REQUEST FORM

      Note that opting out through the above online request form or toll-free number will not affect any third-party cookies, but you can also opt-out of these cookies by following the instructions below.

      How to Opt Back In

      If you would like to opt back into the sale or sharing of your personal information (as applicable depending on your residency), you may do so by contacting Nissan’s Data Privacy line at 1-877-647-2272.

      Proof Required to Opt-Out

      In order to exercise your consumer privacy rights, Nissan is required to verify your identity - in other words, we need to know that you are who you say you are. This is to keep others from exercising your rights without your permission. In order to complete your opt-out, you will need to provide information to prove your identity, such as:

      • First and last name
      • Email address

      Opt-Out and Cookies

      In addition to opting out of the sale or sharing of personal information unrelated to cookies as described above, you can opt-out of the use of cookies by interacting with the cookie consent banner that may appear at the bottom of Nissan websites the first time you come to our site, the “Cookie Settings” link in the footer of our web pages, or by clicking Cookie Settings. You also can opt back into these cookies through the same link.

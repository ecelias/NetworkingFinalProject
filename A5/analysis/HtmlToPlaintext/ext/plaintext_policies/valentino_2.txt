The data we collect and process through the Website, including Electronic Communications, or in our Stores vary. We process your data for various purposes and with different methods, as described below. 
Processing through Valentino S.p. A.
Cookies, The data concerning your browsing of the Website are processed to ensure that the Website works properly and for marketing purposes. Please also refer to our Cookie Policy. The Website uses the following cookies: necessary/Technical Cookies. These cookies enable the Website to function correctly, allowing users to view its contents in the respective language and market from their initial visit. This is the primary purpose of these necessary/technical cookies. They can identify the user's country of connection and ensure automatic redirection to the appropriate version of the Website for their country upon each visit. Additionally, they facilitate account creation, login, and order management. For registered users, the Website can recognize them when accessing services offered exclusively to registered users, thanks to these cookies. Furthermore, these cookies can detect if users purchase products on the Website through affiliated or partner sites such as Tradedoubler and Rakuten, enabling us to fulfill our obligations related to such partner sites (thus fulfilling our commitment linked to the purchase). These cookies are necessary for the Website's operation.
Analytic Cookies, These cookies are utilized to generate statistical analyses concerning how users navigate the Website, including the device used, the quantity of pages visited, or the number of clicks on a specific page during navigation. The outcomes of these analyses are processed anonymously and solely for statistical purposes. Therefore, the purpose of the processing is to produce statistical aggregated analyses regarding the usage of the Website. 
Profiling Cookies, The Website utilizes profiling cookies, which enable the tracking of preferences observed during each visit. This allows for the creation of profiles that facilitate the sending of messages tailored to users' preferences and interests. For instance, it may expedite the process of finding desired products or recommend similar items. Furthermore, these cookies may enhance the existing client profiles within our databases, enabling us to offer personalized services and experiences. The primary purpose of this processing is analysis and profiling, with the legal basis being the user's consent.
Cookies for marketing and retargeting purposes (first and third party), These cookies are employed by trusted first and third parties to showcase banner advertisements on affiliated sites (retargeting), featuring products recently viewed by users. As users navigate the Website, these cookies additionally suggest other products that may pique their interest or items similar to those recently viewed, based on their browsing history. Additionally, these cookies may be used for enhanced conversion activities, where collected data is hashed to ensure privacy. The purpose of this activity is marketing, with the legal basis being the consent provided by the user.
Web Beacons or Tracking Pixels, A web beacon, or tracking pixel, is a small image file on a web page that may be used to collect certain information from your device for the purpose of keeping track of user behavior, web traffic, site conversions, and enhanced conversion. This information may include the IP address, time of access, browser, and identification of cookies. On the Website, we use beacons and tracking pixels for several platforms, including Adobe Analytics, Google Ads, Tik-Tok and Contentsquare. With your consent, we or our vendors, may utilize web beacons to track visitor statistics to the Website and manage cookies, as well as help inform advertisements on third-party platforms. This includes activities related to enhanced conversions, particularly in collaboration with Google, Meta, and TikTok, where hashed user data may be used to improve the accuracy of conversion tracking based on activity tracked from your browser or device. Additionally, these tools help identify return traffic from third-party platforms such as social media applications, and for third-party web analytics. 
Log Files and Browsing Data, This information is sent automatically to us by your browser each time you visit the Website. The transmission of this information is not dependent on the presence or use of cookies and is unaffected by your opt-in or opt-out election concerning cookies.

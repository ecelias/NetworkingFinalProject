To opt-out from such sharing on this website, you may adjust your cookie preferences for Samsung's site by adjusting the toggle below. You can change these settings at any time. 
Global Privacy Control ("GPC") 
You may choose to use a tool to communicate your choices through an opt-out preference signal, such as GPC on your browser or device, if available. Samsung honors GPC signals as a request to opt-out of sharing personal information for purposes of targeted advertising on our website. 
Other Opt-Out Methods 
To see what other opt-out of targeted ads options are available to you, please visit the Samsung Privacy Request Portal.

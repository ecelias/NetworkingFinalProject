2. Shipping methods

Deliveries are free of cost. Estimated delivery time for USA is 2- 6 business days.

Please note that any order made after 1pm (GMT +1) on Friday and throughout Saturday and Sunday will be dispatched for delivery on the Monday of the following week. Spanish and US national holidays, local public holidays in Madrid and United Sates and bank holidays in the shipping destination can also lead to delays in deliveries.

In order to guarantee security and speed, all orders are shipped using high quality courier services. Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a tracking number and a direct link by email that will allow you to check the status of your order and follow its progress at the couriers website.

Deliveries are made between 8am and 7pm and could require a signature to ensure safe and secure delivery of your order. The estimated delivery time is approximate, it does not guarantee delivery on the date indicated.

Please note that we do not ship our Perfumes to any address in the United States.

Check the full list of stores where this service is offered through this link.

You will receive an email confirmation when your order is ready to be collected from the store. Please visit the selected store in within 15 days after this email is sent to collect your order.

Collection yourself you need:

Representative needs:

If you do not collect your order: Your order will be available in-store for 15 days. If not collected, it will be sent back and your original method of payment will be refunded.

7. Exchanges and returns

Exchanges

You may exchange the items purchased from LOEWEs online store within thirty (30) days at our offline stores (except for department stores, outlets, wholesalers and franchises) within the same country of purchase, with the delivery note provided with every purchase, the order confirmation email or the official invoice of your online purchase. The item(s) must be in their original condition and with their original packaging and their corresponding accessories in order to be exchanged.

Exchanges in store can be made for items having equal price or higher price. If you exchange for an item having a higher price, you must pay the price difference.

Exotics, and any other product purchased from the LOEWE online store which has been customized or is part of special pre-order collections, is not subject to the Exchanges and Return policy.

Your legal rights for lack of conformity of products are not affected by this policy.

Returns

Free returns are available within 14 days of your online order delivery date. Returns are only accepted in the same country of the online purchase. Returns of purchases made in loewe.com from 14th November can be requested until the 15th of January.

If you would like to Return an item or items purchased from the LOEWE online store, you may do so within 14 calendar days following the date of delivery.

Returns from the Loewe Online store in the United States are only accepted in the United States. International purchases may not be returned in the United States and must be returned in the country of purchase.

You will be refunded the amount paid for the item(s) within a maximum period of fifteen (15) days of receiving and approving the Return.

An item made from exotic skin, any item purchased from the LOEWE online store that has been customized or personalized for you, any item that is part of our special pre-order collections, or any item whose return is not appropriate on health or hygiene grounds is not subject to this return policy. These items are final sale.

In the case of candles and perfumes, the items must be returned in their original packaging, perfectly sealed and intact in order to guarantee the correct hygienic conditions. Depreciation could also be considered in this case.

In the case of Books or Stationery, the items must be returned in their original packaging, perfectly sealed and intact.

LOEWE will pay for the delivery cost of returning the item(s) to our Central Warehouse.

Simply enter on My Account or click here.


The article(s) must be in perfect condition, and returned in its/their original packaging, along with all the accessories.

LOEWE will refund the amount paid (excluding any return delivery costs) to the card with which you made the payment. Only the original purchaser of the product(s) can request a return as the refund will be directly made to the card with which the payment was made. This process might take up to 15 days.

LOEWE will not be responsible or liable if any item(s) you wish to return to LOEWEs online store is lost, misdirected or delivered late. The customer must bear the risks of shipping their purchase.

LOEWE offline stores do not accept returns of any product(s) acquired from the LOEWE online store.

In the event an item bought online is deemed faulty, LOEWE reserves the right to either repair, replace or refund the item at no additional cost to the customer and user. LOEWE will address non-conformities within a period of 2 years after the date of purchase. Customers and users will have a maximum period of 2 months to report the non-conformity to LOEWE.
